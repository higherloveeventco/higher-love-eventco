The Model

Traditional planning models — day-of, partial, full-service — are built for couples who want to be closely involved in every decision. There's nothing wrong with that. But until now, there hasn't been a model designed specifically for the couple who is overwhelmed, short on time, or simply doesn't want wedding planning to be their part-time job.

Higher Love fills that gap. A fixed planning formula with published pricing and pre-curated options at every turn — including eight complete tablescape collections available to every planning client, with linens, place settings, glassware, candles, florals, and a matching signage suite. No design fee. No starting from scratch. The goal is to minimize decision fatigue and help you feel as calm during planning as you will on your wedding day.

Once your date is locked in, the same process repeats for vendors. Depending on your timeline, we can hire vendors in waves to keep decision fatigue to a minimum.
