Dear Arshad and Najma,

It was a genuine pleasure speaking with you both on Sunday. What you are planning for Umair and Sara is a beautiful and thoughtful celebration, and the care and intention you are both bringing to every detail comes through clearly.

Below is a custom proposal for both events. I have presented two service options for each: full-service planning and coordination, and coordination-only. I want to be straightforward with you about which I recommend and why, because I think it matters for your family.

My recommendation

Full-service planning means I am involved from the beginning: sourcing vendors, reviewing contracts, managing all logistics, and communicating with everyone on your behalf from now through the wedding day. Coordination-only means I step in six weeks before each event, take over what you have already planned, and execute it.

For your family, I would love to be your guide through every part of this. You are planning a beautiful, complex celebration across two cities and two cultures, in a wedding format that is still new to you — and that is a lot to carry. Full-service planning means you do not have to carry any of it. I take care of the vendors, the contracts, the logistics, and every detail in between, and you get to show up and be present for your family. The coordination-only option is there if budget requires it, but my honest recommendation is to let me assist from the beginning. This celebration deserves it, and so does your family.
