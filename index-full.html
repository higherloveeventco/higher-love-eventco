How it works

The big decisions made simple, one step at a time.

No research. No rabbit holes. No blank pages. Just a clear sequence of simple choices — and someone ready to guide you through each one.

01

Pick your package

Choose your celebration type and guest count. That's the only big decision up front.

02

Reserve your decor

Choose from eight pre-designed collections. The look is already curated — no design fee, no separate decorator to hire. Just pick the one that feels right.

03

Pick your venue

I present three venues available for your date, ranked #1 to #3 with honest pros and cons. No FOMO, no rabbit holes.

04

Build your team

A curated vendor shortlist for every category — 1 to 3 ranked picks, personally vetted and matched to your wedding.

And then

I take it from there.

Contracts, logistics, timeline, and day-of command. You show up and enjoy your wedding.
