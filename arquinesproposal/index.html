A note on your budget

Venue and rental costs are closely linked — some venues include tables, chairs, and dishware while others require sourcing everything separately. Once your venue is confirmed, we'll reassess the rental budget together.

The single biggest driver of your budget is guest count. The fewer guests you invite, the more you can spend per person — better food, a better bar, a nicer venue. As your guest count grows, you'll need to find value in every category to compensate, and in this industry, you get what you pay for. Every guest added means more catering, more seating, more florals, more rentals, and higher bar minimums. It's worth having that conversation early — and I'm here to help you think it through.
