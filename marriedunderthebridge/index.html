The experience

A Cathedral Park elopement,
built by two St. Johns studios.

Higher Love Event Co. and Weddinglandia both call St. Johns home. We are neighbors who love this community — the people, the bridge, the parks, the small town feel that somehow survived in the middle of one of the busiest cities in the Pacific Northwest. This package is what happens when two local businesses choose collaboration and community over competition.

Wedding permits beneath the St. Johns Bridge are currently unavailable while bridge repairs wrap up, but we found a way to help you get Married Under the Bridge. Weddinglandia coordinates the elopement: just the couple, two witnesses, and the officiant. Higher Love coordinates the picnic reception for you and up to 18 of your favorite people, the vendor team, and all logistics. The vendor team is Portland local, with most of our partners based right here in St. Johns. One neighborhood, showing up for your wedding day.

How the day flows

Vows, Portraits, and a Picnic.

The structure of this package was designed so both of you can be completely present for every part of it. The ceremony is intimate and legal. The portraits happen while the picnic is being set. By the time you return, your guests are there welcoming you to the celebration.

Portland weather is part of the experience, not a problem to solve. We plan for it in advance.

02

Portraits in the park

While your guests settle into the picnic, you and your photographer walk Cathedral Park. Your guests get to relax. You get the photos.

The picnic awaits

Come back to linen-draped tables, blankets and cushions, florals, candles, and a full spread from She Serves. The wine is already poured. You sit down and enjoy it.

The setup

How the picnic is set up.

Cathedral Park has two picnic tables set close together, each seating up to 10. Those anchor the whole setup. Florals, charcuterie, candles, and place settings share the surface. If the spread needs more room, a third vintage-style table comes in as overflow so the main tables stay comfortable.

The sundries

A generous spread from She Serves.

Charcuterie, fruits, nuts, breads, and sweets, all sourced and arranged by She Serves. Your choice of PNW wine from 45th Parallel, local craft beer from Stormbreaker Brewing, or a mix of both. Seasonally infused water served from a drink dispenser. Everything is laid out and ready before you return from portraits. Vegan options available.

The atmosphere

Abundant, leisurely, celebratory.

Linen runners, candlelight, and perfectly mismatched place settings. Florals from Still City Flowers woven in among the food and candles. Fruits, nuts, and breads nestled between arrangements. The kind of table that looks like it took no effort at all.

Comfort is key

Tables for everyone, lawn for those who want it.

We use picnic tables rather than a ground spread because we believe every guest should be comfortable and included. Blankets, cushions, and rugs are on the lawn for those who want to settle in closer to the ground. If you have any accessibility needs, please let us know and we will do everything we can to accommodate.

Entertainment

Music, games, and a camera to remember it by.

A Bluetooth speaker set to your playlist. One lawn game of your choice set up and ready for your guests. A Polaroid camera with film so the afternoon gets documented in the best possible way. Included in The Garden Party and The Soiree.

Weather policy

We plan for Portland weather.

This package is designed for Portland's temperate seasons. A little rain or an overcast sky is not a reason to cancel. We have a clear rain plan in place and you will always know what is happening before 8:00am on your wedding day.

A light rain or overcast sky is part of the experience, not a problem to solve.

If conditions call for it, the decision to move indoors is made jointly by Higher Love Event Co. and Weddinglandia. You will be notified right away.

Rain plan

Your picnic moves to Weddinglandia.

If it rains, the picnic reception moves indoors to the Weddinglandia chapel the same day. Your ceremony, florals, food, and everything included in your package stays exactly the same.

Rescheduling

Available for genuine safety concerns.

Packages are non-refundable and non-transferable. Rescheduling is available if conditions make travel or outdoor activity genuinely unsafe, such as a declared weather emergency or road closures.

Rain, clouds, or cooler temperatures on their own are not grounds for rescheduling.
