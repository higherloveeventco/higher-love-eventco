Every collection includes

What's on every table.

Every collection is built around the same foundation. What changes is the aesthetic: the colors, textures, and finishing details that make each look its own.

The table

  • Full-length table linen
  • Coordinating runner
  • Coordinating napkins
  • Table number frame

Each place setting

  • Charger plate
  • Dinner plate
  • Water goblet
  • Dinner fork and knife

The centerpiece

  • 6 bud vases with florals
  • 6 votive candles
  • Taper or pillar candles, matched to the collection

Matched templates

  • E-invitation template
  • Wedding website template
  • Canva signage templates suite: welcome sign, seating chart, table numbers, bar and catering menus

Larger floral arrangements for the head table and welcome table are available as an add-on.

Custom decor

Have something specific in mind?

If none of the eight collections match your vision, a custom tablescape is available. Custom means starting from scratch: a design built around your specific aesthetic, venue, and budget, sourced and styled by us.

Custom decor is designed collaboratively. We'll talk through your vision, source the elements, and build a look that's entirely your own. Custom decor is quoted separately after an initial consultation.

To get started, reach out through the contact page and mention that you're interested in custom decor.

What custom decor includes

  • A full tablescape design built around your wedding
  • Custom sourcing of all decor elements
  • Delivery, setup, styling, and teardown
  • Coordination with your venue on logistics
