Sustainability

Sustainability is part of the formula.

Your wedding should feel good. So should everything it leaves behind.

Rentals over disposables

Nothing single-use if I can help it.

Every tablescape collection is sourced through a rental partner. Linens, flatware, glassware, and centerpieces are returned, cleaned, and used again. No landfill contribution from your table settings.

Food waste

Leftovers go to people who need them.

Any food remaining at the end of the night is donated to St. Johns Village Transitional Housing Complex, right here in the neighborhood.

Florals

Your flowers have a life after the reception.

Floral arrangements are donated to Assumption Village Assisted Living in St. Johns after the wedding. A centerpiece that spent 6 hours on a reception table can spend another week brightening a room for someone who needs it.

Sourcing locally

Food and flowers, grown close to home.

I work with caterers who source ingredients locally and florists committed to locally grown flowers. If a bloom can be substituted for a locally grown alternative without compromising the look, I'll do it.
